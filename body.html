Greetings from the homeland of wine, Georgia!

We're a proud family-owned business nestled in the beautiful Kakheti region of Georgia, where the rich traditions of winemaking have been cherished for centuries. We wanted to reach out and introduce you to our exceptional product that combines tradition, technology, and the magic of Rostom Saginashvili's golden voice.

What Sets Us Apart:

In our pursuit of crafting the finest Georgian wines, we adhere to the age-old traditions of winemaking. Our secret ingredient? Qvevri – those large earthenware vessels that have been the heart and soul of Georgian winemaking for generations.

Introducing Our Unique Gift Box:

Our pride and joy is our gift box, which includes three exquisite 0.75-liter bottles of wine, each with its own unique character and flavor. But what truly sets this gift box apart are the custom-made QR codes adorning each bottle.

Unlocking a World of Stories:

When you scan these QR codes, you're transported to a custom-made webpage that we've designed with love. This webpage doesn't just tell the story of our wines; it also delves into the inspiration behind this line – the legendary Rostom Saginashvili, known as the "golden voice" of Georgia.

The Voyager 2 Connection:

Did you know that Rostom's voice was so enchanting that NASA sent it deep into space aboard the Voyager 2 mission? The exact song that journeyed into the cosmos can be experienced by scanning the QR codes on our bottles. It's a truly cosmic connection that makes our wines even more special.
